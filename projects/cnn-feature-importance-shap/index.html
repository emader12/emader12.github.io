Feature Importance Analysis with Explainable AI

Using SHAP to Identify Key Spectral Regions for NASA's Habitable Worlds Observatory

This project, completed during my NASA Ames Research Center internship, applies explainable AI techniques to understand how convolutional neural networks identify important features in exoplanet atmospheric spectra. By combining deep learning with SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) values, I developed a framework to compare data-driven feature importance with traditional physics-based information content methods.
